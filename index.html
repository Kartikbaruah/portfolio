About Me

A few years ago, I was immersed in a corporate role as an accountant in Assam. While the job offered stability, my passion for technology and coding led me to discover a new path—web development. What began as a hobby soon turned into a fulfilling career, and I decided to leave my successful (yet often stressful) accounting career behind to pursue my true calling—building websites and applications.


I'm a lifelong learner, constantly seeking to challenge myself and expand my skill set. Whether it's mastering new programming languages, diving deep into React.js, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, PHP, or MySQL, or exploring the latest trends in web development, I’m always eager to grow. I also find inspiration in nature, finding balance and creativity in the great outdoors, which fuels my passion for innovation and continuous improvement.
Alongside my technical skills, I pride myself on being a passionate problem solver, always eager to take on new projects and create engaging, user-centric experiences. Check out some of the exciting projects I’ve worked on below!
